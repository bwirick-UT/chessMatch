Chess Controls

Camera Controls

  • Orbit: Right-click + drag
  • Zoom: Mouse wheel

Game Controls

  • Select Piece: Left-click on a piece
  • Move Piece: Left-click on a highlighted square
  • Cancel Selection: Click on a different piece or ESC

Game Information

  • The current player and game status are shown in the top-left corner
  • Valid moves are highlighted when you select a piece
  • The camera automatically rotates to the current player's perspective
  • One piece at random will rotate and follow your mouse pointer (no bearing on gameplay)
